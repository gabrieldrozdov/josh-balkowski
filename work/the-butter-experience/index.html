Butter

Butter is all about bringing restaurant-quality dining into the home with an intimate, elevated experience built around warmth, craftsmanship, and connection. Through refined branding, art direction, and photography, a visual language was crafted that feels both inviting and elevated. A minimalist logo, typography, and colour palette set the tone, while photography captured the “I want an invite!” energy. The website was designed as a seamless extension of the experience, alongside supporting collateral like business cards and pricing sheets to keep the brand cohesive at every touchpoint.
