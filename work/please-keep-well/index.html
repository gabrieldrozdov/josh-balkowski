Please Keep Well

A second life looks good on you.

T-Shirts

Please Keep Well started with the goal of repurposing by giving thrifted pots a new, reusable life as homes for Ben’s homegrown plants. Their first run of merch follows the brands environmentally conscious perspective. Each design blends Ben’s hand-drawn sketches with repurposed content from their Instagram. A loud bark, with absolutely no bite, all designs will be screen-printed in limited runs (on thrifted tees, of course)—some day soon.
